  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    1. cheddar
    2. mozzarella
    3. pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
Preparation steps:
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with a second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
